Helping Others Brings Joy
助人為快樂之本

CEF A2 Level Children's Story (No Illustration)

1. English: Ruby is a little rabbit who lives in a beautiful forest.

中文：Ruby 是一隻住在美麗森林裡的小兔子。

2. English: She loves to play with her friends every day.

中文：她每天喜歡和朋友們一起玩。

3. English: One morning, Ruby sees a small bird sitting under a tree.

中文：一天早上，Ruby 看到一隻小鳥坐在樹下。

4. English: The bird looks sad because its wing is hurt.

中文：小鳥看起來很難過，因為牠的翅膀受傷了。

5. English: Ruby walks closer and asks, “Can I help you?”

中文：Ruby 走近並問：「我可以幫助你嗎？」

6. English: The bird smiles and says, “Yes, please.”

中文：小鳥微笑並說：「好的，謝謝你。」

7. English: Ruby carefully takes the bird to her little house.

中文：Ruby 小心地帶小鳥回到自己的小屋。

8. English: She gives the bird some food and clean water.

中文：她給小鳥一些食物和乾淨的水。

9. English: Ruby also helps clean and protect the bird’s hurt wing.

中文：Ruby 也幫忙清理並保護小鳥受傷的翅膀。

10. English: After a few days, the bird becomes healthy again.

中文：幾天後，小鳥恢復健康了。

11. English: The bird can fly in the blue sky again.

中文：小鳥可以再次在藍色天空中飛翔。

12. English: The bird thanks Ruby for her kindness.

中文：小鳥感謝 Ruby 的善良。

13. English: Ruby feels very happy because she helps a friend.

中文：Ruby 感到非常快樂，因為她幫助了一位朋友。

14. English: She learns that helping others brings joy to her heart.

中文：她明白幫助別人能帶給自己心中的快樂。

15. English: From that day on, Ruby always helps animals in need.

中文：從那天起，Ruby 總是幫助需要幫忙的動物。

16. English: The forest becomes a warmer and happier place because of her.

中文：因為她，森林變成一個更溫暖、更快樂的地方。
